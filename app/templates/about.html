A first-of-its-kind café in the GCC, blending Asian-inspired snow ice, fruit slush, and creamy milk teas with our signature handcrafted jellies and flans. Every dessert is crafted to be fun, flavorful, and Instagram-worthy — giving you a multi-sensory experience in every bite.

Company Overview

Albee Resto Café will be a food truck offering a unique food experience with a fusion of traditional Middle Eastern flavors and contemporary international cuisine. Our mission is to provide high-quality, innovative, and affordable snacks and desserts to the residents of Public park Dubai community especially kids who are coming to Public play area of Dubai..

Mission

To introduce a fun, flavorful, and visually stunning dessert experience never seen before in the GCC, combining creativity, premium ingredients, and unforgettable textures.

Vision

To become a beloved community gathering spot known for quality food, impeccable service, and sustainability.

Concept & Cuisine

Offering innovative snow ice, slush, and creamy milk tea topped with our signature handcrafted jellies and flans — a revolutionary dessert experience in the GCC.
